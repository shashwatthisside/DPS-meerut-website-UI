Warm Welcome To DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL MEERUT

ABOUT SCHOOL

Delhi Public School Meerut is committed to the promotion of education and human values. It strives to seek out the positive, to enhance inherent skills and to empower the physical, emotional and intellectual faculties through comprehensive quality education incorporating global trends with the traditional Indian ethos. The realization resulted in the inception of Delhi Public School Meerut in the year 2002 & got affiliated from the Central Board of Secondary Education in due course.

AN ODE TO PERSEVERANCE

SHRI M.P. SINGH

"Every great institution is the lengthened shadow of a single man. His character determines the character of the organization ".

Shri M.P. Singh was born on the 1st of May, 1947 in village Partapur in Meerut District of Uttar Pradesh in a middle-class agricultural family. After completing his elementary education from his native village, Shri M.P. Singh pursued his graduation in Mechanical Engineering from Kurukshetra University. He was a F.I.E. and Chartered Engineer.Shri M.P Singh always had a strong urge to do something for the welfare of the society, especially, the weaker sections.
